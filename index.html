About me

Cameron McBride

Hello, my name is Cameron McBride, and I'm 28 year old digital media specialist. I was born in Chattanooga, Tennessee, moved on up north to a small town Duxbury, Massachusetts(Go Red Sox!). I grew up playing sports and learning how to play percussion, guitar, and bass guitar. I was always interested in creating some form of art. Early in my college career, I wanted to be a performing musician, but didn't know if that's what I truly wanted to do. I took my first digital media class in 2011 and instantly fell in love with it. During this class, we were being taught about web development. I then learned about HTML and CSS and couldn't get enough of it.
